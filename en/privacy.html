Privacy Policy - Orbis

Last updated: May 12, 2026 · Portugues

1. Introduction

Orbis ("we", "our" or "the app") is an AI-powered astrology application that respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what your rights are under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD - Law 13.709/2018), and other applicable regulations.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account data

  • Email address (authentication)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile picture (optional, provided by the social login provider)

2.2 Sensitive astrological data

To generate your birth chart and personalized interpretations, we collect:

  • Date of birth
  • Time of birth (when provided)
  • Place of birth (city, state, country, and geographic coordinates)

This data is used exclusively to calculate planetary positions, astrological houses, and to generate interpretations. It is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

2.3 App usage data

  • Mood tracker entries (when you choose to log them)
  • Conversation history with the Orbis AI
  • User preferences (language, theme, notifications)
  • PRO subscription data (status, renewal date)

2.4 Technical data collected automatically

  • Anonymous device identifier
  • Device model, operating system, and app version
  • Error and performance logs (via Sentry)
  • Anonymized usage events (via PostHog)
  • Advertising identifiers (via AdMob, free plan only)

3. Device Permissions

Orbis requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: to send your daily horoscope, lunar phase alerts, and relevant transits (optional, you may disable at any time)
  • Billing (Google Play Billing): to process the PRO subscription

Orbis does not access camera, gallery, contacts, calendar, or real-time device location.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your active session
  • Calculate your birth chart and generate personalized interpretations
  • Provide contextual responses in the AI chat
  • Personalize content (horoscope, transits, influences)
  • Process PRO subscription payments
  • Display relevant ads (free plan only)
  • Improve the app via aggregated usage analytics
  • Detect and fix technical failures

5. Legal Bases for Processing

  • Consent: collection of sensitive astrological data and chat history
  • Contract performance: authentication and provision of the subscribed service
  • Legitimate interest: security, fraud prevention, product improvement
  • Legal obligation: tax records of payments

6. Third-Party Services

Orbis uses the following data processors:

  • Clerk: authentication and session management (servers in the US)
  • Google Cloud / Vercel: infrastructure hosting (US/EU)
  • Google Gemini API: AI generation of horoscopes and chat (US)
  • Neon (PostgreSQL): primary database (US)
  • RevenueCat: subscription management (US)
  • Google AdMob: ad display (free plan only)
  • Sentry: error monitoring (US/EU)
  • PostHog: usage analytics (US)

All processors are bound by data processing agreements compliant with GDPR and LGPD.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with:

  • Service providers listed in section 6, strictly to provide the contracted service
  • Competent authorities, when required by law or judicial order
  • Third parties, with your express consent, when applicable

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: while your account is active
  • Birth chart and astrological data: until you delete your account
  • Mood records: up to 24 months
  • AI chat history: up to 12 months or until manual deletion by the user
  • Crash logs: up to 90 days
  • Financial records: 5 years (legal obligation)

9. Data Security

We adopt technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest in the database
  • Authentication via secure OAuth 2.0 / OIDC tokens
  • Database access restricted by access control

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Confirm the existence of processing of your data
  • Access your data
  • Correct incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data
  • Request anonymization, blocking, or deletion of unnecessary data
  • Request data portability
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data processed with your consent
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority (ANPD in Brazil, your local authority elsewhere)

To exercise any of these rights, send an email to orbis.suporte@gmail.com with the subject "Privacy Request".

11. Account Cancellation and Deletion

You can delete your account at any time by sending an email to orbis.suporte@gmail.com with the subject "Deletion Request - Orbis". Details of the process, data that will be removed, and timelines are on our account deletion page.

After confirmation, your personal data is removed within 30 days, except for records we must retain by legal obligation (financial records of purchases, retained for 5 years).

12. Children

Orbis is intended for users 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a legal guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

13. International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers (Clerk, Google, Sentry, PostHog, RevenueCat) operate on servers located outside Brazil, mainly in the United States and the European Union. We ensure these transfers are protected by adequate legal mechanisms, as required by GDPR and LGPD.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. When there are material changes, we will notify you within the app or by email. The last updated date is shown at the top of this document.

15. Data Protection Officer (DPO) and Contact

For questions related to privacy, data protection, or to exercise your rights:

Email: orbis.suporte@gmail.com
