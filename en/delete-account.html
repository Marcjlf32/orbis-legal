How to request deletion of your account and data

You can request the complete deletion of your Orbis account and all associated data at any time.

Send an email to:

orbis.suporte@gmail.com

In the subject line, write:

Deletion Request - Orbis

In the body of the email, include:

  • The email linked to your Orbis account
  • Confirmation that you wish to permanently delete the account

Data that will be deleted

  • Account identification (email, name)
  • Birth data (date, time, and place)
  • Calculated birth chart (planetary positions, houses, ascendant)
  • Mood tracking history
  • Conversations with the Orbis AI astrologer
  • App preferences and settings
  • Push notification token

Data that may be retained

  • Anonymized security logs (up to 30 days)
  • Financial purchase records (5 years, Brazilian tax requirement)

Timeline

Your request will be processed within 30 days of email confirmation.

Partial deletion

If you want to delete only specific items (e.g., chat history with the AI astrologer) without removing your entire account, include that request in the same email.
